About Me

Hi, I'm Aleira!

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at Cornell University, pursuing a Master of Engineering with a concentration in robotics. My academic work has given me hands-on experience in developing algorithms, sensor fusion techniques, and control systems for autonomous platforms. I recently earned my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering at Cornell in May 2025 and immediately began my M.Eng. program as an early admit. I look forward to completing my master’s degree this December. My career interests span mechatronics, software engineering, automation, automotive systems, aerodynamics, and ride engineering. I am also drawn to roles that involve travel and collaboration across different environments. Having been born and raised on an island, I’ve grown used to flying more often than driving, and I hope to bring that same sense of adaptability and exploration into my professional journey.
